FAQ

Who is eleQtron?

eleQtron GmbH was founded in 2020 as a spin-off of the Department of Quantum Optics at the University of Siegen and is home to the first German quantum computer. The core business of the start-up of founder Prof. Dr. Christof Wunderlich, Dr. Michael Johanning and Jan Leisse is the development and operation of future-oriented quantum computers based on stored ions.

What makes eleQtron so special?

eleQtron is the first manufacturer of quantum computer hardware in Germany. Our MAGIC technology is groundbreaking and will make it possible to provide computing time on quantum computers for industrial applications. We are a highly qualified international team and have already been able to convince major investors with our project, which is funded by the German Federal Ministry of Education and Research.

What are eleQtron's corporate goals?

Our goal is to become the leading provider of quantum computing time. With an order volume of over €50 million and experienced investors at our side, we will bring a freely programmable and scalable quantum computer to market.

Why is it worth investing in eleQtron?

Because quantum computing is the technology of the future! The market potential is already huge and will grow even more in the coming years. With our MAGIC technology, we have succeeded in creating a groundbreaking innovation that will enable us to open up new markets. In doing so, we will focus on highly relevant and lucrative sectors of the economy.

Chemicals, pharmaceuticals and automotive (materials research), logistics (route and network optimizations) and finance (risk management, portfolio optimizations) as well as increasingly important fields such as machine learning are just a few examples.

Dr. habil. Michael Johanning –
Gründer & CTO

Der habilitierte Physiker Dr. Michael Johanning ist der CTO von eleQtron und Namensgeber unseres Konzepts MAGIC App. Neben seiner erfolgreichen Mitwirkung an einer Vielzahl von Drittmittelprojekten hat er etliche Forschungsbeiträge zur Quanteninformationstechnologie veröffentlicht. Dr. Johanning blickt unter anderem auf eine Karrierestation bei Prof. Dr. William Phillips (Nobelpreis 1997 für Laserkühlung) zurück und war vor seinem Vollzeit-Einsatz in unserem Team als Dozent an der Universität Siegen tätig.

Jan Leisse – CEO

Unser Geschäftsführer Jan Leisse ist Diplom-Ingenieur mit zusätzlichem MBA. Er hat langjährige Erfahrung im Turnaround-Management und Corporate Development und war zuvor Geschäftsführer beim Maschinenbauspezialisten Albrecht Bäumer. Mit seinem prozessorientierten Arbeiten ist er die treibende Kraft hinter dem schnellen Wachstum von eleQtron.

Kaoru Naganuma –
CPO

Als Chief Product Officer bei eleQtron ist Kaoru Naganuma für die Schnittstelle zwischen Technologie, Produktstrategie und Unternehmensvision verantwortlich. Er verfügt über mehr als 15 Jahre Erfahrung im Silicon Valley und in der europäischen Automobilindustrie. Bei Tesla, Apple und Mercedes-Benz hat er globale Software- und Produktinnovationen vorangetrieben.
Bei Tesla in Palo Alto war er als Staff Product Manager tätig, berichtete direkt an den VP of Software und Elon Musk und leitete die Entwicklung der Software für die Modelle 3, S und X. Nun bringt er seine Mischung aus Silicon-Valley-Geschwindigkeit und fundiertem technischen Fachwissen in den Bereich Quantencomputing ein und verwandelt visionäre Forschung in skalierbare, praxistaugliche Produkte.

Dr. habil. Michael Johanning –
Founder & CTO

Dr. Michael Johanning, a physicist with a postdoctoral degree, is the CTO of eleQtron and the namesake of our MAGIC app concept. In addition to his successful involvement in a number of externally funded projects, he has published several research papers on quantum information technology. Dr. Johanning looks back on a career with Prof. Dr. William Phillips (Nobel Prize 1997 for laser cooling) and was a lecturer at the University of Siegen before joining our team full-time.

Jan Leisse – CEO

Our Managing Director Jan Leisse is a graduate engineer with an additional MBA. He has many years of experience in turnaround management and corporate development and was previously Managing Director at mechanical engineering specialist Albrecht Bäumer. With his process-oriented work, he is the driving force behind eleQtron’s rapid growth.

Kaoru Naganuma –
CPO

As Chief Product Officer at eleQtron, Kaoru Naganuma shapes the intersection of technology, product strategy and corporate vision. He has over 15 years experience in Silicon Valley and the European automotive industry, and has driven global software and product innovation at Tesla, Apple and Mercedes-Benz.
While at Tesla in Palo Alto, he served as a Staff Product Manager, reporting directly to the VP of Software and Elon Musk, and led the development of the software for the Model 3, S and X. He now brings his blend of Silicon Valley speed and in-depth engineering expertise to quantum computing, transforming visionary research into scalable, real-world products.
